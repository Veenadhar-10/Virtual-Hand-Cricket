🏏 Hand Cricket – Rules ❌

  • This is a 2-innings game between you and the computer (bot).
  • You can choose how many overs you want to play.
  • If no overs are selected, the default will be 1 over (6 balls per innings).
  • Each innings has a maximum of 6 × (number of overs) deliveries.
  • You only have 1 wicket – if you're out, your innings ends immediately.

🎮 Gameplay

  • Every ball, both you and the bot choose a number from 1 to 6.
  • If the numbers are different:
    ➤ The batter scores the number they chose.
    (e.g., you pick 4, bot picks 2 → you score 4)
  • If the numbers match:
    ➤ The batter is OUT! and the innings ends immediately.
  • After one innings, the other player bats and tries to chase the target.

🏆 Result

  • The player with the higher score after both innings wins.
  • If both players end up with the same score, it’s a draw.
  • In the second innings, the game ends immediately if the chasing player beats the target.

⚠️ Notes

  • Only numbers from 1 to 6 are valid – no 0, extras, or bonus balls.
  • The bot picks its number randomly on every ball.
  • To close this rules section, click “❌” at the top right.
